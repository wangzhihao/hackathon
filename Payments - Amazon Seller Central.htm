    Payments Summary

    The Payments report displays your account's payment information. This report makes it possible to do the following:

    • View an account summary that includes beginning balance, next scheduled date for transfer of funds, and order and refund totals.
    • View credits, fees, and adjustments for specific orders.
    • Download a settlement report containing all your transactions for a date range you select.
    • If available, view the Federal Automated Clearing House (ACH) Trace ID assigned to your ACH fund transfer. The Federal ACH Trace ID can be used by your bank’s ACH specialist to trace funds that have not appeared in your bank account after 5 business days from the time that the transfer is initiated. The Federal ACH Trace ID is assigned to ACH transactions only and available for up to 90 days after the fund transfer date.
    Note: If you are signed up for both the Seller Central Selling on Amazon program and the Checkout by Amazon program (for selling on your own site), then you will have two separate settlement reports.

    Transactions View

    This page displays a summary of account transactions that have occurred since the last settlement period, up until the prior day's close. A transaction can be an order, a refund, or an Amazon-initiated charge or credit.

    You can modify the transaction summary view using the filter and search options.

    Filter view by

    Use this option to choose the type of transactions to display:

    • All transaction types (default)
    • Order Payment
    • Refund
    • Chargeback Refund: Buyers initiate refunds through their credit card company.
    • A-to-z Guarantee Refund: For more information see the A-to-z Guarantee.
    • Service Fees
    • Shipping services purchased through Amazon
    • Paid to Amazon
    • Other

    Within

    Use this option to choose the date range of transactions to display:

    • Statement period: This is the default. It includes all transactions from the settlement period.
    • Past number of days: Choose from pre-set numbers of days.
    • Custom date range: Choose your own date range.

    To download order and payment information from the Transaction View page, including listing and line item details not displayed directly on this page, use the Download button. Learn More about Payments Reports.

    Search for a transaction

    Select this option, enter an order ID, and click Go to display transactions for that specific order.

    Transaction Details

    To view the details for a specific transaction, click the link in the Total column on the Transaction View page. The Transactions Details page displays specific details for one transaction and lists any related transactions, such as refunds related to an order transaction.

    Note: The Transaction Details page displays payment information only. To research or refund orders, click view details of this order.

    If you are signed up for both Selling on Amazon and Checkout by Amazon (for selling on your own site), you will have two separate Payments reports.
